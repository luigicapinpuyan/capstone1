My Songs

Face of Mine

I wrote this song to express my feelings of a lack of identity; I often try to conform to those around me, and I used to be a lot more afraid of showing how I really felt and of saying what I really wanted to say. This is my most successful song yet!

Georgie

I came up with the guitar riff for this song first. I tried to think of 6-word phrases that fit the riff (since it has 6 notes), and one of them was "I love you, but not now". My roommates and I agreed that this sounded like a parent talking to their child. I, on the other hand, remembered sometimes feeling annoyed at my dog when he'd try and catch my attention, and felt the guilt of not being able to spend more time with him. I decided to write the rest of the lyrics with this topic in mind. Personally, it's my favorite among the three listed here.

Human

I came up with the riff first, again. I didn't quite feel like I was in control however; this was one of those songs that kind of just write themselves. Around 90% of the lyrics are what immediately came to mind; I didn't quite know what I was singing about until I finished the song. I decided to name it human, since, in my opinion, the common factor among the lyrics are emotions everyone has probably felt in their lifetime.
